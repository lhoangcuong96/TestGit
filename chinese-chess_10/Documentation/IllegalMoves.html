Illegal Moves

Legal moves are indicated with white dots. HiddenLynx uses the following color coding for illegal moves:

  • O Yellow - Move is prohibited because the kings would see each other
  • O Blue - Move is prohibited because it would produce a perpetual check
  • O Orange - Move is prohibited because it would produce a perpetual chase
  • O Cyan - Move is prohibited because it would produce a perpetual situation

In addition to the colors, a textual explanation is given or the kings blink to give the reason why a move is illegal.
